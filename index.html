Andy Warhol Stamp


Commemorative postage stamp featuring
one of Warhol's self-portraits.


The U.S Postal Service issued an 18-inch stamp in 2002, as a tribute
to Andy Warhols achievements. The official unveiling ceremony by the
United States Postal Service was held at the Andy Warhol Museum.
